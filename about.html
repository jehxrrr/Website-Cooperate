Background & History

Masjid Ar Rahimah Desa Pandan serves as the community mosque for Muslims around Desa Pandan and nearby residential areas in Kuala Lumpur.


The mosque was established to provide a comfortable space for daily congregational prayers and Friday prayers, as well as serving as a center for religious learning that continues to grow alongside the rapid development of Desa Pandan.

Quick Facts

  • Location: Jalan 4/76B, Desa Pandan, 55100 Kuala Lumpur.
  • Category: Kariah Mosque (Federal Territory).
  • Capacity: Can accommodate up to 2,000 congregants.
  • Key Services: Daily Prayers, Friday Prayers, Religious Classes, Community Programs.

Vision & Mission

Vision
To become a prosperous, inclusive, and knowledge-based center of worship to strengthen faith and unity within the Desa Pandan community.


Mission

  • Provide quality facilities for worship and religious education.
  • Organize continuous programs for youth, families, and senior citizens.
  • Cultivate caring neighborhood values through community projects.

Center of Worship
